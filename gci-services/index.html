About GCIServices

GCIServices was established in 2016 by a group of professionals in the field of construction and welding. We are engaged in welding work of any complexity. Our main activities are argon arc manual and orbital welding.

GCIServices performs installation and piping connection of process equipment in food, chemical and pharmaceutical industries. Our company also performs repairs, warranty and post-warranty maintenance of various industrial equipment. Basic principles of our work are: high quality welding, shortest possible terms of activities, customer orientation and focus on a final result.

Cooperation with "GCIServices" has the following advantages:

  • Explore design sketches and follow all customer requirements
